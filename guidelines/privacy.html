Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you contribute to CU Archive

Your Privacy Matters

This Privacy Policy explains how CU Archive ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform to contribute educational materials. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care.

Last Updated

This policy was last updated on February 8, 2023. We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Information We Collect

We collect different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you.

Information Type What We Collect Purpose Required/Optional
Contributor Information Name and email address Attribution and communication Optional (name), Optional (email)
Submission Metadata Course, subject, year, semester, paper details Archive organization and categorization Required
Uploaded Content Question papers and reference materials Educational archive maintenance Required
Technical Information IP address, browser type, device information Security, analytics, and platform improvement Automatically collected
Usage Data Submission timestamps, interaction data Service optimization and analytics Automatically collected

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes related to operating and improving CU Archive:

Attribution

To credit you as the contributor (if you provide your name) on the paper's download page and contributors list.

Communication

To notify you about your submission status, updates about the archive, and respond to your inquiries.

Archive Management

To organize, categorize, and make educational materials accessible to students and researchers.

Platform Improvement

To analyze contribution patterns, identify popular subjects, and improve our submission process.

Security

To monitor for fraudulent activity, protect against security threats, and ensure platform integrity.

Legal Compliance

To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes when required.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in these limited circumstances:

Public Attribution

Your name (if provided) will be publicly displayed alongside your contribution as "Contributed by [Your Name]".

Legal Requirements

If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request to protect our rights or safety.

Service Providers

With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our platform under strict confidentiality agreements.

Academic Research

Anonymized, aggregated data may be shared for academic research purposes to study educational trends.

Anonymous Contributions

You can choose to contribute anonymously by not providing your name. In this case, no personal information will be associated with your submission.

Your Rights

As a contributor, you have certain rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

Request a copy of your personal information we hold and how we're using it.

Right to Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.

Right to Anonymity

Request removal of your name from attribution while keeping your contribution in the archive.

Right to Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information, subject to archive integrity requirements.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@cuarchive.example.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Data Retention

We retain different types of information for different periods based on their purpose:

Submitted Papers

Retention: Indefinitely

Educational materials remain in the archive as long as the platform exists to serve students.

Contributor Information

Retention: As long as the paper remains in the archive

Your name (if provided) is retained for attribution purposes while your contribution is available.

Technical & Usage Data

Retention: Up to 2 years

Analytical data is retained for platform improvement and then anonymized or deleted.

Communication Records

Retention: 3 years

Email correspondence is retained for reference and then securely deleted.

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

Encryption

Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission using TLS encryption.

Access Controls

Strict access controls limit who can view personal information to authorized personnel only.

Regular Audits

Regular security audits, vulnerability assessments, and system updates.

Secure Storage

Secure storage with encryption for sensitive information and regular backups.

Security Limitations

While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information.
