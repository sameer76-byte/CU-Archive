Welcome to Our Guidelines Hub

Before submitting any materials to CU Archive, please review our guidelines. Following these guidelines ensures your contributions are processed quickly and helps maintain the quality of our archive for all students.

These guidelines are designed to help you understand what we're looking for and how to prepare your submissions for the best chance of approval.

Quick Contribution Tips

Before you submit, keep these essential tips in mind:

Check First

Search the archive to ensure the paper isn't already available under a different name or category.

PDF Format

Always submit PDF files when possible. They're our preferred format for all question papers.

Clear Scans

Ensure scanned documents are clear, legible, and properly oriented. Blurry scans will be rejected.

Complete Information

Provide all required details: course, subject, year, and any additional relevant information.

If you have questions that aren't covered in our guidelines, or need assistance with a specific submission, please contact our support team.
