Report Missing Question Paper

Help us improve CU Archive by letting us know which papers we're missing. Your reports help make the archive more complete.

Check First

Before reporting, please use the search function to make sure the paper isn't already in our archive under a different name or category.

Be Specific

The more details you provide, the easier it is for us to find and add the missing paper. Include course, subject, year, and any other relevant information.

Get Notified

Provide your email if you'd like to be notified when the paper you reported is added to the archive (optional).

What Happens After You Report

Here's what you can expect after submitting a missing paper report:

1
Report Received
We'll send you a confirmation email (if provided) within 24 hours.
2
Verification
Our team verifies the paper doesn't already exist in the archive under a different name.
3
Sourcing
We attempt to source the paper from our network of contributors and university resources.
4
Addition to Archive
If found, the paper is added to the archive within 1-4 weeks, depending on availability.
5
Notification
You'll receive an email notification when the paper is available (if you provided your email).

Note: While we make every effort to source missing papers, we cannot guarantee that all reported papers will be found and added to the archive.
