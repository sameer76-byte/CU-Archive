About Chemistry SEC Papers

Skill Enhancement Courses (SEC) for Chemistry are designed to provide practical laboratory and analytical skills alongside theoretical knowledge. Each SEC course carries 2 credits and includes both theory and practical components.

Paper Format

All papers are in PDF format and stored in Google Drive for easy access.

Exam Duration

Theory: 2 Hours | Practical: 2 Hours (separate papers)
