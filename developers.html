Meet Our Development Team

The passionate students behind CU Archive — building tools to help fellow students succeed

Our Mission

CU Archive was created as a student-led initiative to address the challenges students face in accessing previous year question papers and study materials. Our goal is to make educational resources more accessible, organized, and useful for exam preparation.

We believe in open access to education and are committed to building tools that help students learn more effectively. This project is built with passion, late nights, and lots of coffee!
